Frequently Asked Questions

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 15-day money back guarantee. You can sign up to try out our features and get a full refund if you decide it’s not for you.

What is the difference between Pro and Plus?

ArtStation Pro has all the features as ArtStation Plus with some additional features related to selling on digital products and prints, heightened visibility and Pro Analytics. See the full list.

What happens if I cancel my Pro subscription but want to resubscribe in the future?

With the special offer, you can lock-in your Pro plan for $6.99/mo. If you cancel and resubscribe after October 1, 2019, you will have to purchase Pro starting at $9.95/mo.
