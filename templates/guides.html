Resbrotherx Guides

The Resbrotherx Guides are built on information provided by experienced, professional Developers/artists along with statistics from the Resbrotherx community. The Resbrotherx team has gone through the legwork of asking Developers/artists the big questions, collecting data and information, and then organizing it all into easy-to-read guides.

The Resbrotherx Guide to Going from Student to Professional Developer/Artist

The guide will show you how to go from the top of your class to the top of the resume pile. Transitioning from student to professional can be confusing, thanks to the help of artists Alex Beddows, Clinton Crumpler, Jacob Norris, Jonathan Hemmens, Kieran Goodson, Kim Aava, Marcel Schaika, you’ll be able to channel all your hard work and creativity into getting hired!

The Resbrotherx Guide to Freelancing

Becoming a successful freelance is difficult, many of the hurdles are just not knowing where to start. How much should you charge? How do you find work?
Don’t worry, with the help of amazing freelancers Alex Beddows, Randall Mackey, Michael Kinsey, Hannah Amy Watts, Clinton Crumpler, Finnian MacManus, and Taylor Brandenburger, Resbrotherx has put together “The Resbrotherx Guide to Freelancing”.
